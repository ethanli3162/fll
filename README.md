# ANCIENT VISION:

Abstract:

Archaeologists face many challenges in their work cataloging and recording delicate, important artifacts. Two major challenges in recording artifacts are the time-consuming and error-prone manual labeling and storage processes needed for every artifact. Because archaeologists find around 25,000 artifacts at a single site, almost 95% of them are tiny, fragile flakes, documenting these artifacts is extremely difficult. We created Ancient Vision, an end-to-end solution, to overcome these challenges, an end-to-end solution using a hardware scanner that creates artifact replicas quickly and reliably. A custom software platform controls the scanning process, and a secure, locally hosted database stores, sorts, and retrieves artifact information and metadata. It can also connect with other applications, such as reconstruction software. We have researched that around 51,000 archaeologists and museum specialists, +1,000 CRM firms, and 146 major archaeological institutes in the U.S. alone and many more worldwide could benefit from Ancient Vision.
