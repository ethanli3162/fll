# ANCIENT VISION ABSTRACT:

Archaeologists face many challenges in their work cataloging and recording artifacts. Combined with the delicate artifacts and their importance, cataloging them is an extremely difficult job. One major challenge in recording artifacts is the time-consuming and error-prone manual labeling process. Because archaeologists find around 25,000 artifacts at a single site, with almost 95% of them being tiny flakes, documenting artifacts is extremely difficult. We knew there had to be a better way to complete this task, so we created Ancient Vision, an end-to-end solution using a hardware scanner that creates artifact replicas quickly and reliably, a software platform that controls the scanning process, and a secure, locally hosted database that stores, sorts, and retrieves artifact information. It can also connect with other applications, such as reconstruction software. We have researched that around 51,000 archaeologists and museum specialists, 1,000+ CRM Firms, and 146 major archaeological institutes in the U.S. alone and many more worldwide could benefit from our scanner.
