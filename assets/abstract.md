# ANCIENT VISION ABSTRACT:

Archaeologists face many challenges in their work because of the methodology used to learn about our past. Combined with the delicate artifacts and their importance, archaeology is an extremely difficult job. One major challenge in archaeology is the time-consuming and error-prone manual labeling process. Because archaeologists find around 25,000 artifacts at a single site, with almost 95% of them being tiny flakes, documenting artifacts is extremely difficult. We knew there had to be a better way to complete this task, so we built Ancient Vision, an end to end solution that creates accurate digital copies of artifacts using a hardware scanner that captures artifact information quickly and reliably, and a software platform that controls the scanning process. A secure, cloud-based database stores, sorts, and retrieves artifact metadata. Ancient Vision's database can also connect with other applications, such as reconstruction software. We have researched that around 51,000 archaeologists and museum specialists, 1,000+ CRM Firms, and 146 major archaeological institutes in the U.S. alone and many more worldwide could benefit from our scanner.
